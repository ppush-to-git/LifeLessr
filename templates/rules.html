How to Play

LifeLessr challenges you to identify the real-world inspiration behind your favorite games, anime, movies, music, and more.

Gameplay

  • Study the image carefully.
  • Click on the map where you think the game inspiration/origin location is.
  • Submit your guess to reveal the answer.
  • Complete all 4 rounds to finish the game.

Inspiration vs Origin

Some media are inspired by real-world locations, while others are entirely fictional.

  • Inspiration – The real-world place that inspired the setting.
  • Origin – The location where the media was created.

If a title has no meaningful real-world inspiration, your guess will be compared against its origin instead.

Tips

  • Not every answer is a city—some are regions or entire countries.
  • Close guesses are rewarded, so don't worry about pinpoint accuracy.
  • Use clues from architecture, landscapes, vehicles, signs, and landmarks.

Good luck, and have fun exploring!
